Where education meets
technology

A revolutionary E-ink tablet that will digitise the entire education system and make it more accessible.

Features

Everything you need for your school.

Firstly

Students will be able to make notes, add comments in textbooks and also customize their notes. They will have access to store as much as they would like with the option to opt for more storage if their devise memory is full.

Secondly

Students will be able to easily download books for their classes and will also have access to educational videos and articles, newspaper, educational games etc.

And last but not the least

Students will be able to submit queries to their teachers and also attend tests or even exams just from their tablet. The will also be able to do group discussions and submit their assignments and projects as well.

Benefits

Everything available quicker, easily and with less carbon footprint on our environment.

Less paper waste.

Out of 17 billion cubic feet of trees deforested each year, over 60% are used to make paper. This deorestation is responsible for nearyly a fifth of worl's annual greenhouse gas emissions. With turning digital although the environmental impact is not not negligible but it is enough to make an impact.

Unlimited Space.

Store as many notes, tests, screenshots etc. And when the storage is full we will have the option of additional space available on cloud with charges. Instead of spending money on books you might have to get more space but we promise it will be less than the cost of your books. :)

Tests, Exams and what not

Our devise will not only benefit students but also teacher, this devise is for everyone in the education system and that is whyy we like to call it revolutionary. Teachers will be able to create quizes, tests and even conduct exams on these devises. It would be easier for them to check assignments as they will not have to handle loads of paper and notebook from students. As this devise progress we are also going to add attendance sheet in this as well which would help to store all the information for a student in a cloud and will be easily accessible to evaluate a student as their past rescords will be available as well.
